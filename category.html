Tesla Heavy Rocket Catch

The Tesla rocket refers to the SpaceX Falcon Heavy rocket, launched by Elon Musk's company, SpaceX. It is one of the most powerful operational rockets, capable of carrying large payloads into space. The rocket gained significant attention in 2018 when a Tesla Roadster was launched into space as a test payload.

Tesla Robot

The Tesla Robot, also known as **Tesla Optimus**, is a humanoid robot designed by Tesla to perform tasks that are dangerous, repetitive, or boring for humans. Unveiled by Elon Musk in 2021, its goal is to handle labor-intensive tasks in various industries, including manufacturing and home assistance. Powered by Tesla's AI and advanced robotics, it is intended to improve productivity and reduce labor costs, while also contributing to solving global labor shortages.
